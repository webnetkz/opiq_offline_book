WHAT IS CORTISOL

Cortisol is your body’s main stress hormone, but it does way more than manage stress response.

It is a “multi-tasking” hormone, meaning it controls many bodily functions…

  • Fuels your body’s “fight-or-flight” instinct
  • Boosts energy levels
  • Regulates mood
  • Controls sleep/wake cycles
  • Helps control blood sugar levels
  • Manages how your body uses carbs/fats/protein
  • Regulates metabolism
  • Helps reduce inflammation

If you are mentally stressed out, have an “a-type” personality, do not get enough sleep, are calorie restricted for an extended time, or are training too hard – there is a good chance your cortisol could be out of range.

Your body NEEDS healthy levels of cortisol, but too much can lead to a host of issues.
