15. Sweat-Proof Headphones

Gym-goers know too well how much of a pain it is when you drench your headphones in sweat. Plus, regular headphones are too quick to pop out during moderate to heavy exercise sessions.

One of the best workout gifts you can give a fitness buff is a new pair of sweat-proof headphones. Make sure to get one that can withstand intense sweat sessions of HIIT, CrossFit, and strength training.

16. Yoga Mat

A great workout gift for your friends who do calisthenics or yoga sessions is a yoga mat. Nothing’s worse than getting dirt and grime all over your sweat-coated body.

With a yoga mat, they can lie down on the floor as much as they want. Plus, it’s not expensive. You can get these exercise gifts for just a few bucks.

One of the coolest fitness gift ideas for your protein-addicted friend is a shaker bottle. If you drink protein shakes, you know you can never have too many shaker bottles.

For example, a set of face towels is affordable yet very useful. Your friend will surely appreciate functional workout gifts like these.
